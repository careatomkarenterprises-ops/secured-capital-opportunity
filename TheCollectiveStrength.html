What happens to my money if something goes wrong?

From day one, you hold a Post-Dated Cheque (PDC) for your FULL principal amount. If we ever miss a payment or breach the agreement, you have the legal right to deposit that cheque. Your principal is never at risk—it's secured by the banking system itself.

How do I know you'll actually pay on time?

We have 2+ years of verifiable payout history—month by month, client by client. Every single payout has been made between the 1st and 10th of the month. No exceptions. We can share this proof with you privately on request.

Can I withdraw my money before 12 months?

This is a 13-month structured agreement (12 payouts + principal refund in month 13). Early withdrawals are not part of the standard terms. However, in genuine hardship cases, we evaluate on a case-by-case basis. The PDC security ensures your principal is always protected.

Why do you pay 10-18% on trials? Is it too good to be true?

We call this our "Trust-Buying Budget." We deliberately pay higher on small trial amounts so you can verify our punctuality and process risk-free. Once trust is established, we transition to sustainable 5% monthly for larger capital (₹10L+). The high trial rate is our cost of earning your confidence.

Who do I sign the agreement with? The company or an individual?

You sign a notarized loan agreement on ₹500 stamp paper with our Executive Director personally. This makes the agreement personally enforceable while being backed by the corporate structure. You receive the original notarized copy.

What exactly is a PDC? How does it protect me?

A Post-Dated Cheque (PDC) is a cheque dated for a future date. At the time of agreement, you receive a cheque from us for your FULL principal amount, dated 13 months later. You hold this cheque safely. If we honor the agreement, you return it. If we don't, you deposit it—the bank honors it, and your money is recovered.

Do I have to pay tax on these payouts?

Yes, the interest income is taxable under "Income from Other Sources." We provide you with a yearly statement of payouts. TDS is not deducted as this is a private agreement; you are responsible for declaring it in your ITR. Consult your CA for exact treatment.

What if I want to invest more after the trial?

After successfully completing a trial, you're eligible for our Standard Tier (5% monthly for ₹10L+ deployments). Many of our current partners started with the ₹50k trial and scaled up after seeing 3-4 months of punctual payouts.
