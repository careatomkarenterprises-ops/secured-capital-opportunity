Your Questions Answered

How does the first payout work for March?

If capital is transferred on March 18th, earnings start from March 19th. You receive a pro-rated payout for 13 days (March 19-31) on 1st-10th April. This is approximately 42% of the monthly payout (13/31 days). Then, from April onwards, you receive full monthly payouts (credited between 1st-10th of each month) for the remaining 11 months.

What are the document options?

Digital (IT Act 2000): ₹2,00,000 - Email agreement, digital approval.

Notarized: ₹2,04,999 - ₹500 stamp paper, physical documents couriered, notarized copy delivered.

Both include PDC security under NI Act 1881.

How is my capital secured?

You receive a Post-Dated Cheque (PDC) for the full ₹2,00,000 under NI Act 1881, Section 138. In case of payment default, the cheque can be deposited. The agreement is legally enforceable.

When are payouts credited?

All payouts are credited between 1st and 10th of each month. The first payout (pro-rated for March) is credited in April. Subsequent payouts are credited by the 10th of each month.
