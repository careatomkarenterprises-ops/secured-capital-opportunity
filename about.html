Santosh Shendkar

Founder & Managing Partner

With over a decade of experience in private lending and financial partnerships, Santosh Shendkar has established Omkar Enterprises as one of Maharashtra's most trusted private investment platforms. His journey began in 2015 with a simple vision: to create transparent, secure, and rewarding investment opportunities outside the volatile traditional markets.

Under Santosh's leadership, Omkar Enterprises has maintained a 100% payout record since inception, delivering consistent 1% monthly returns to over 500 satisfied partners. His hands-on approach ensures every investor receives personal attention, complete transparency, and the security of legally notarized agreements with PDC protection.

Santosh believes in the power of trust and relationship-based investing. "When you invest with Omkar Enterprises, you're not just a client - you're a partner in our growth journey," he says. This philosophy has created a community of investors who benefit from both financial returns and the peace of mind that comes from working with a founder who stands behind every commitment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the 1% monthly return guaranteed with Omkar Enterprises?

Yes, 1% monthly returns are contractually obligated in our notarized bilateral agreement under Indian Contract Act 1872. We provide Post-Dated Cheques (PDCs) for the entire investment period as security for these returns.

How is my investment principal secured?

Your principal is secured through multiple layers: (1) Post-Dated Cheques (PDCs) issued for the full investment amount, (2) Legally notarized agreements, (3) PAN/Aadhar verification of all parties, (4) Regular financial audits and transparency, and (5) Direct access to founder Santosh Shendkar.

What is the minimum investment amount?

We offer flexible investment tiers: Basic Tier (₹1-5 lakhs), Premium Tier (₹5-20 lakhs), VIP Tier (₹20-50 lakhs), and Enterprise Tier (₹50+ lakhs with custom terms). All tiers receive the same 1% monthly returns.

How long has Omkar Enterprises been operating?

Omkar Enterprises has been operating since 2015 with a 100% payout record. We've served 500+ satisfied partners with consistent monthly returns throughout this period.
