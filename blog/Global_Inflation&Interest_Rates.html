üõ°Ô∏è Risk Management Education

Proper risk management is the most important skill any investor can learn. Here are key principles for educational purposes:

Key Principles Every Learner Should Know

  1. Capital Preservation: Never risk more than you can afford to lose - this is rule number one
  2. Position Sizing: Proper allocation based on individual risk tolerance and account size
  3. Stop Loss Concepts: Understanding protective measures to limit potential losses
  4. Diversification: Spreading risk across different assets, sectors, and geographies
  5. Risk-Reward Ratio: Calculating potential returns against possible risks before any decision
