Important Market Insight: Alert Mode, Not Panic Mode

We are sharing this not to cause panic, but to ensure everyone is alert.

  • The Nature of the Move: In global markets, significant events and sharp moves like this are often used by "Big Money" to book profits or create liquidity. These movements can be purposefully accelerated to trigger panic selling among retail investors.
  • The Strategy: Do not let fear dictate your decisions. If you are holding quality positions, panicking and selling at the bottom benefits institutional players, not you.

📌 The Action Plan: Be smart, not fearful.

  • Watch the Support: The level of 23,666 (Nifty Future) is crucial. Observe how the market reacts here.
  • Averaging Strategy: For existing holdings, consider averaging only if the price takes support at these levels and sustains for 3 to 4 working days. Sustained consolidation above a support level often indicates strength and can lead to a bounce.
  • If Support Breaks: Be aware that if the 23,666 level is broken with high volume and fails to recover, it could trigger a sharper fall. In that scenario, protecting capital becomes the priority.

Stay Calm and Stay Smart. Events like this are designed to make you sell. Don't fall into the trap. Stick to your risk management, watch how the price behaves around these key levels, and act rationally rather than emotionally.
