🧨 5 Volume Patterns That Can Improve Your Trading

🚀 Rocket Launch

High volume + Price up → Real buying.

💀 Ghost Pump

Low volume + Price up → Likely trap.

🧨 Climax Top

Extreme volume + Price flat/down → Smart money distribution.

🕳️ Iceberg

High volume + Price down → Panic, then opportunity.

📈 Silent Accumulation

Average volume + Sideways → Big players accumulating.

📘 Beginner Guide: How to Check Volume in Zerodha, Groww & TradingView

  • Zerodha Kite: Open chart → volume bars at bottom → add 20-day volume moving average from "Studies".
  • Groww: Stock chart → Indicators → search "Volume".
  • TradingView India: Default volume at bottom; add OBV or Volume Profile.

📊 Advanced Volume Indicators: OBV & Volume Profile

On Balance Volume (OBV): Adds volume on up days, subtracts on down days. Rising OBV confirms uptrend.
Volume Profile: Shows volume at specific price levels. Identifies support/resistance zones.
