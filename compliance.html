BUDS ACT 2019 COMPLIANT

Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act 2019

The Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act, 2019 specifically regulates deposit-taking activities. Our model is exempt from BUDS Act regulations as we operate as a B2B private lending partnership with limited partners, not as a deposit-taking entity.

Why We Are Exempt from BUDS Act

  • B2B Model: We operate as Business-to-Business partnerships, not accepting public deposits
  • Limited Partners: We work with a limited number of sophisticated partners (less than 50)
  • No Public Solicitation: We do not publicly advertise or solicit deposits from the general public
  • Not a Collective Investment Scheme: Each partnership is individual, bilateral, and customized
  • No Guaranteed Returns: Returns are performance-based with PDC security, not guaranteed deposits
  • Registered Partnership: We are a registered partnership firm under Indian Partnership Act 1932

Key Distinction

We are NOT accepting "deposits" as defined under Section 2(4) of BUDS Act. We are entering into secured lending partnerships with select business partners under Indian Contract Act 1872, which is specifically exempted from BUDS Act regulations for B2B transactions.
