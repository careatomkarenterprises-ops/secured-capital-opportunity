Legal Framework Overview

Omkar Enterprises operates within the complete legal framework of Indian laws. We are a registered partnership firm (Registration Number available upon request) operating since 2015. Our model is designed to be fully compliant with all applicable Indian laws.

Primary Governing Laws

  • Indian Contract Act, 1872: All partnerships are structured as bilateral loan agreements under Section 10
  • Indian Partnership Act, 1932: We operate as a registered partnership firm
  • Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881: Post-dated cheques (PDCs) are issued under this act
  • Information Technology Act, 2000: Digital communications and transactions compliance

Reference: Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India

Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes (BUDS) Act 2019 Compliance

FULLY COMPLIANT

The Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act, 2019 specifically regulates deposit-taking activities. Our model is exempt from BUDS Act regulations because:

Why We Are Exempt from BUDS Act

  • B2B Model: We operate as Business-to-Business (B2B) partnerships, not public deposits
  • Limited Partners: We work with a limited number of sophisticated partners (less than 50)
  • No Public Solicitation: We do not publicly advertise for deposits
  • Not a Collective Investment Scheme: Each partnership is individual and bilateral
  • No Guaranteed Returns: Returns are performance-based with security

BUDS Act 2019, Section 2(4): Exclusions for B2B transactions

Key Distinction: We are NOT accepting "deposits" as defined under Section 2(4) of BUDS Act. We are entering into secured lending partnerships with select business partners.

RBI Guidelines Compliance

RBI GUIDELINES FOLLOWED

While we are not an NBFC (Non-Banking Financial Company) requiring RBI registration, we voluntarily follow RBI's fair practices guidelines:

  • Transparency: Complete disclosure of all terms and conditions
  • Fair Practices: No hidden charges or unfair terms
  • Grievance Redressal: Established mechanism for partner concerns
  • KYC Compliance: Full KYC documentation for all partners
  • Security: PDCs provide legal security to partners
