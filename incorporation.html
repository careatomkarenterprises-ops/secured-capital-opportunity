Established Excellence

The Legacy of Strategic Growth.

Omkar Enterprises was founded on a singular realization: that the stock market is the world's most powerful wealth creator, yet it remains inaccessible to many due to time constraints and market complexity.

We didn't start to compete with the market; we started to master it for those who are busy building their own legacies in jobs and businesses.

— Santosh Shendkar, Founder & Market Analyst

Who We Are

Managed by Santosh Shendkar, a veteran market analyst with over 15 years of experience, Omkar Enterprises is the financial arm of TRFSK Omkar Services Pvt. Ltd.

Our Philosophy

We specialize in Equity Opportunity Holding. Unlike short-term trading that carries high stress and volatility, we focus on identifying long-term value and holding positions until the market reflects their true worth.

Our approach combines rigorous market analysis with patience, allowing us to capitalize on opportunities that others might miss in the daily market noise.
