Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about private lending partnerships with Omkar Enterprises

Omkar Enterprises operates as a private lending partnership firm under the Indian Contract Act, 1872. We are not a SEBI-registered investment advisor, bank, or NBFC. All partnerships are bilateral agreements between consenting adults with proper legal documentation, notarized agreements, and PDC (Post Dated Cheque) security.

Returns are generated through our proprietary private lending model where funds are deployed in secured business opportunities with strong collateral. The 1% monthly return is contractual and guaranteed through PDCs (Post Dated Cheques) provided at the beginning of the partnership. We maintain a 100% payout record since 2015 by carefully selecting lending opportunities and maintaining adequate liquidity reserves.

Your investment is protected by multiple security layers:

  • Complete set of PDCs covering entire investment period
  • Legally notarized partnership agreement
  • PAN/Aadhar verification of all parties
  • Regular financial audits and transparency
  • Direct access to founder Santosh Shendkar
  • Monthly statements and payment confirmations

We offer flexible investment tiers to suit different financial goals:

  • Basic Tier: ₹1,00,000 to ₹5,00,000
  • Premium Tier: ₹5,00,000 to ₹20,00,000
  • VIP Tier: ₹20,00,000 to ₹50,00,000
  • Enterprise Tier: ₹50,00,000+ (Custom terms)

All tiers receive the same 1% monthly returns, with personalized service scaling with investment amount.

Starting your investment journey is simple:

  1. Schedule a meeting with founder Santosh Shendkar (in-person in Ulhasnagar/Pune or virtual)
