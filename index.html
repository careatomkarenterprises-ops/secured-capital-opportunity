Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our partnership model

Is the 2% monthly return guaranteed?

Yes, it is contractually obligated in our notarized bilateral agreement. We have maintained 100% payout punctuality since 2018 with zero missed payments. The 2% monthly finance charge is a contractual obligation, not a market-linked return.

How is my principal secured?

We issue Post-Dated Cheques (PDC) for your full principal amount at the time of agreement signing. These cheques remain in your possession as direct banking security. In case of any default, you can directly present these cheques to your bank for encashment.

What happens in case of delay in monthly payout?

Our agreement includes a 90-day grace period for business continuity. However, we have maintained perfect payout timing since inception. In the unlikely event of delay, you have the right to encash the PDCs after the grace period. This civil protection ensures no criminal proceedings are involved.
