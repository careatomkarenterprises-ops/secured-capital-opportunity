Important Risk Disclosure

Past Performance Notice

Historical returns of 2% monthly (24% annually) are based on our performance from 2018-2025. These results don't guarantee future performance. Market conditions, economic factors, and regulatory changes may affect future returns.

Security Mechanisms

While PDCs (Post Dated Cheques) provide a layer of security for your principal amount, they don't eliminate all investment risks. PDC encashment depends on bank clearance and sufficient funds at maturity date.

Regulatory Framework

Omkar Enterprises operates as a registered partnership firm under Indian Partnership Act, 1932. We're not registered with SEBI as portfolio managers or investment advisors. Our services constitute private lending arrangements between consenting parties.

Suitability Assessment

Our investment model is suitable for individuals who:

  • Have emergency funds separate from investment capital
  • Understand private lending risks
  • Can tolerate potential delays in liquidity
  • Have consulted with their financial advisor
