Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our partnership model

What are the monthly returns and when are they paid?

We provide 1% fixed monthly returns credited on the 1st of every month without fail. Additionally, we offer performance bonus which is distributed between the 2nd and 10th of each month based on business performance.

How is my principal secured?

We issue Post-Dated Cheques (PDC) for your full principal amount at the time of agreement signing. These cheques remain in your possession as direct banking security. In case of any default, you can directly present these cheques to your bank for encashment.

What happens if returns are delayed?

Our agreement includes a 90-day grace period for business continuity. However, we have maintained perfect payout timing since inception. The 1% fixed returns are guaranteed on the 1st, and performance bonus follows by 10th. In the unlikely event of delay, you have the right to encash the PDCs after the grace period.
