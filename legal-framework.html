PDC Security System

Post Dated Cheques provide complete protection for your principal investment amount

How PDC Security Works

Cheque Collection

We collect Post Dated Cheques covering your full principal investment amount at the beginning of partnership.

Secure Storage

PDCs are stored in bank lockers with dual custody. They are never deposited unless absolutely necessary.

Monthly Returns

You receive 2% monthly returns via bank transfer on the 1st of every month, independent of PDCs.

Principal Protection

At partnership end, PDCs are returned uncashed. If returns stop, you can deposit PDCs to recover principal.

PDC Security Benefits

  • Complete principal protection
  • Legally enforceable security
  • Bank guarantee equivalent
  • Quick recovery mechanism
  • Standard banking instrument
  • Court recognized security

RBI Compliance & Exemption

Understanding our position within the Reserve Bank of India regulatory framework

Private Lending Exemption

Omkar Enterprises operates under the RBI's exemption for private lending between individuals and partnership firms.

Status: Exempt from NBFC registration under RBI Act Section 45-IA

Partnership Structure

As a registered partnership firm, we facilitate bilateral lending partnerships between investors and businesses.

Status: Compliant with Partnership Act 1932

Regulatory Position Summary

Omkar Enterprises operates as a registered partnership firm facilitating private lending arrangements. We are exempt from NBFC registration under RBI regulations as we don't accept public deposits. Our model involves bilateral agreements between consenting parties with full disclosure and legal documentation. All transactions are documented and reported as per income tax regulations.
