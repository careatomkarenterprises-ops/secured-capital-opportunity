PDC Security System

Post Dated Cheques provide complete protection for your principal investment amount under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act

How PDC Security Works

Cheque Collection

We collect Post Dated Cheques covering your full principal investment amount at the beginning of partnership as security for legally enforceable debt.

Secure Storage

PDCs are stored in bank lockers with dual custody. They are never deposited unless absolutely necessary for principal recovery.

Monthly Returns

You receive 1% fixed monthly returns via bank transfer on the 1st of every month, with voluntary performance-based top-ups, independent of PDCs.

Principal Protection

At partnership end, PDCs are returned uncashed. If returns stop, you can deposit PDCs under Section 138 to recover principal amount.

PDC Security Benefits Under Section 138

  • Complete principal protection
  • Legally enforceable security under Indian law
  • Bank guarantee equivalent security
  • Quick recovery mechanism through banking channels
  • Standard banking instrument recognized nationwide
  • Court recognized security with established legal precedent
