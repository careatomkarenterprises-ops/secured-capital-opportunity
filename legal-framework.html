Legal Framework & Security Protocols

Transparency through contractual certainty and regulatory compliance

Nature of Agreement Security Mechanisms Key Terms Regulatory Compliance Dispute Resolution

1. Nature of Agreement

Clause 1.1 - 1.5

Bilateral Loan Agreement

All partnerships with Omkar Enterprises are structured as Private Bilateral Loan Agreements under the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

Not a Public Deposit

This arrangement is strictly a private agreement between Omkar Enterprises and the partner. It does not constitute a public deposit, collective investment scheme, or banking activity.

No SEBI/RBI Regulation

As a private bilateral agreement, this partnership is not regulated by SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) or RBI (Reserve Bank of India).

Exempt from BUDS Act 2019

This arrangement is exempt from the Banning of Unregulated Deposit Schemes Act, 2019 as it qualifies as an inter-corporate deposit between business entities.

2. Security Mechanisms

Clause 2.1 - 2.8

Multi-Layer Protection

Post-Dated Cheques (PDC)

Issued for the full principal amount at agreement signing. These cheques remain with the partner as primary security.

Enforceable under: Section 138 of Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881

Notarized Agreement

Legally binding contract signed in presence of a notary public. Contains all terms, conditions, and obligations of both parties.

Personal Guarantee

Directors provide personal undertaking for business obligations, adding an additional layer of security.

3. Key Contractual Terms

Clause 3.1 - 4.5

90-Day Grace Period

To ensure business continuity, Omkar Enterprises maintains a 90-day written notice period for principal withdrawal requests.

Purpose: Business stability & capital rotation

Monthly Returns

2% fixed monthly finance charges paid via NEFT/IMPS on agreed dates. Performance bonuses are discretionary.

Minimum Tenure

8-month minimum lock-in period from the date of agreement execution for all partnership tiers.

Early Withdrawal

Premature withdrawal results in recalculation at 1.5% monthly rate for the actual period, with excess adjusted against principal.

4. Regulatory Compliance

Annexure A

Important Regulatory Status

Omkar Enterprises operates under the following regulatory framework:

EXEMPT
SEBI Regulations

Not a collective investment scheme (CIS) as defined under SEBI (CIS) Regulations, 1999.

EXEMPT
RBI Guidelines

Not an NBFC (Non-Banking Financial Company) and does not accept public deposits.

COMPLIANT
Indian Contract Act, 1872

All agreements are executed under and governed by the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

EXEMPT
BUDS Act 2019

Exempt as per Section 2(4) - "amount received in the course of, or for the purpose of, business"

5. Dispute Resolution

Clause 8.1 - 8.7
1

Written Notice

Any dispute must be raised via written notice with 30 days for resolution attempt.

2

Mediation

If unresolved, parties agree to mediation by a mutually agreed third party.

3

Arbitration

Final binding arbitration in Pune/Thane jurisdiction under Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

Important Legal Notes:

  • All disputes are civil in nature only
  • No criminal proceedings can be initiated unless fraud is established in civil court
  • Liability is limited to company assets only
  • Personal assets of directors are protected
