Our Story: A Decade of Trust

Founded in 2015 by Santosh Shendkar, Omkar Enterprises began with a simple philosophy: "Build wealth together through transparent partnerships." What started as a small private lending operation in Ulhasnagar has grown into a respected partnership firm serving 500+ partners across Maharashtra.

Our Founding Principle

"We believe true wealth is created through partnerships where both parties grow together. Our success is measured by our partners' success."

— Santosh Shendkar, Founder
