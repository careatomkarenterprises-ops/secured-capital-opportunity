Privacy Policy

At Omkar Enterprises, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information with utmost confidentiality. This policy outlines our strict data protection measures for investment partners.

1. Information We Collect

Omkar Enterprises collects minimal necessary information strictly for investment partnership management and legal compliance:

Personal Identification

Name, address, contact details, PAN Card, Aadhar Card for KYC compliance

Financial Information

Bank account details, investment amount, transaction history, partnership agreements

Documentation

KYC documents, notarized partnership agreements, PDC details, legal documents

Communication Records

Emails, phone records, meeting notes strictly related to investment partnership

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for legitimate partnership purposes:

  • Partnership Management: Establishing and managing your investment partnership, processing monthly returns, maintaining financial records
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with regulatory requirements including KYC norms, tax regulations, and partnership laws
  • Communication: Sending important updates, return statements, and partnership-related information
  • Service Enhancement: Improving our services based on anonymous partner feedback (no personal data shared)
  • Security: Protecting against fraud, unauthorized transactions, and ensuring partnership security

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement robust, multi-layered security measures:

Physical Security
All physical documents stored in secure, access-controlled environments with biometric access
Digital Security
Electronic data encrypted with AES-256, secure servers with firewalls, intrusion detection
Access Control
Strict role-based access, limited authorized personnel, audit trails for all data access

4. Information Sharing Policy

We maintain strict confidentiality and limited information sharing:

No Commercial Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Legal Requirements

Information may be disclosed only when required by law, court order, or government regulatory authorities.

Service Partners

Limited information shared with trusted banking partners and legal advisors strictly for partnership execution.

With Consent

Information shared with third parties only with your explicit written consent for specific purposes.

5. Data Retention Policy

We follow strict data retention guidelines:

  • Active Partnerships: Data retained for partnership duration + 7 years for financial record keeping
  • Legal Requirements: Mandatory retention as per Indian Partnership Act and Income Tax Act
  • Secure Disposal: Data destruction using approved methods (shredding, secure deletion) after retention period
  • Right to Erasure: You may request data deletion subject to legal retention requirements

6. Your Rights as a Partner

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access Right
Request copies of your personal data we hold
Correction Right
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
Objection Right
Object to certain data processing activities
Deletion Right
Request deletion of personal data (subject to laws)

7. Cookies & Website Tracking

Limited Website Use: Our website (if accessed) may use essential cookies for functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or collect personal data through website analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Age Restriction: Our investment partnership services are available only to individuals aged 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we immediately delete the information.

9. Policy Updates

Regular Review: This privacy policy is reviewed annually. Significant changes will be communicated to existing partners through email or registered post. Continued partnership after updates constitutes acceptance of revised policies.
