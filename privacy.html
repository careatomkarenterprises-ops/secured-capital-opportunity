Data Protection Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal necessary information for investment partnership management:

  • Personal Identification: Name, address, contact details, PAN Card, Aadhar Card
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, investment amount, transaction history
  • Documentation: KYC documents, partnership agreements, PDC details
  • Communication: Emails, phone records, meeting notes related to partnership
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, device information for portal access (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Partnership Management: To establish and manage your investment partnership, process returns, and maintain records.

2.2 Legal Compliance: To comply with regulatory requirements including KYC norms and tax regulations.

2.3 Communication: To send important updates, return statements, and partnership-related information.

2.4 Service Improvement: To enhance our services and partner experience (anonymous data only).

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

Physical Security

All physical documents are stored in secure, access-controlled environments with limited personnel access.

Digital Security

Electronic data is encrypted and stored on secure servers with firewalls and intrusion detection systems.

Access Control

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access partner information on a need-to-know basis.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 No Commercial Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authorities.

4.3 Service Providers: Limited information may be shared with trusted service providers (banking partners, legal advisors) strictly for partnership purposes.

4.4 With Consent: We share information with third parties only with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Active Partnerships: Data is retained for the duration of partnership and as required for ongoing management.

5.2 Post-Partnership: After partnership conclusion, we retain necessary records as required by law (typically 7-10 years).

5.3 Secure Disposal: Data no longer required is securely destroyed using approved methods.

5.4 Right to Erasure: You may request data deletion subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Your Rights

As our partner, you have the right to:

Access Information

Request copies of your personal data

Correction Rights

Request correction of inaccurate data

Objection Rights

Object to certain data processing

Deletion Rights

Request deletion of personal data

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website (if applicable) may use cookies for functionality and analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser. We do not use cookies for tracking personal information beyond website usage analytics.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete it.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated to partners. Continued partnership constitutes acceptance of updated policies.
