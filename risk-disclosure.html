Past performance is not indicative of future results. While Omkar Enterprises has maintained perfect payout records since 2015, there is no guarantee that future performance will be similar. All investments carry inherent risks.

Key Risk Factors

Understand these important risk factors before investing:
  • Market & Business Risks: The 1% fixed monthly returns and performance bonus are dependent on underlying business performance. Economic conditions, market fluctuations, and business operations can affect returns.
  • Liquidity Risk: All investments have an 8-month minimum tenure with a 90-day notice period for principal withdrawal. Early withdrawals may result in reduced returns at 0.75% instead of 1%.
  • Legal & Regulatory Risk: This is a private B2B arrangement under Indian Contract Act 1872. Changes in regulatory framework could affect partnership terms and operations.
  • Security Risk: While secured by Post-Dated Cheques, cheque bouncing/legal recovery involves time and legal processes. PDC security provides recourse but not instant liquidity.
  • Concentration Risk: Your investment is concentrated with a single entity. Diversification is recommended across different investment types.
  • Inflation Risk: Returns may not keep pace with inflation, affecting real purchasing power of your investment over time.

Our Risk Mitigation Measures

We implement several measures to mitigate investment risks:
