Tier Selection FAQ

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. The additional capital will be covered under a supplementary agreement, and returns will be calculated from the date of additional investment.

What happens if I want to invest between tiers?

Investments are categorized based on the amount. For example, ₹75,000 falls under Silver Tier, while ₹1,50,000 falls under Gold Tier. The returns are calculated accordingly.

Is there a maximum investment limit?

The maximum investment per partner is ₹25,00,000 under Platinum Tier. For larger investments, we offer customized corporate partnership structures.
