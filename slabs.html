Tier Selection FAQ

Common questions about choosing the right investment tier for your needs.

How do I choose between Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers?

The main factors are your investment amount and desired level of service. Silver is ideal for first-time investors, Gold offers the best value with performance bonuses, and Platinum provides premium services for larger investments.

Is the Post-Dated Cheque security the same for all tiers?

Yes, all tiers are equally secured by Post-Dated Cheques (PDC). The security mechanism is identical regardless of investment amount.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher tier at any time by investing additional funds. The returns will be calculated based on the new total investment amount.

What happens after the 8-month minimum tenure?

After 8 months, you can choose to continue your investment, withdraw your principal, or reinvest. All options are available with no penalties after the minimum period.
